# LUMIO Privacy Policy (Gizlilik Politikası)

**Effective Date:** [24/02/2026]  
**Last Updated :** [24/02/2026]

LUMIO ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data LUMIO may collect, how it is used, and how it is protected when you use the LUMIO mobile application ("App").

LUMIO is a meal planning and kitchen assistant app that includes features such as daily recipe suggestions, snack suggestions, baby kitchen recipes, weekly planning, shopping list support, nutrition/analysis summaries, fridge-based recipe matching, and the LUMI Chef experience.

> **Important note:** LUMIO does **not** require account creation or login to use the core features of the app.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information you directly provide
LUMIO does not require you to create an account and does not ask for personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number for normal app usage.

However, when using the app, you may enter content such as:
- Ingredients you add to **Buzdolabım (My Fridge)**
- Weekly meal planning selections
- Shopping list tracking/check states
- In-app preferences and usage-related inputs

This information is generally used to provide app functionality.

---

### 1.2 Information stored locally on your device
To provide core features, LUMIO may store certain data locally on your device (for example, using local app storage), including:
- Weekly meal plan data
- Shopping list and checked/unchecked item states
- Fridge ingredient entries
- App preferences
- Premium/entitlement status cache
- Usage counters / feature limits
- Some in-app progress or assistant-related states (such as badges/streak-like progress, if applicable)

This local data helps the app function properly and personalize your experience **on your device**.

---

### 1.3 Subscription / Premium purchase-related data
LUMIO offers optional premium features via **Google Play** and/or the **Apple App Store**.

To verify premium access and entitlement status, LUMIO may process limited purchase-related information such as:
- App-generated user identifier (non-login app user ID)
- Platform (Android / iOS)
- Product ID / subscription ID
- Transaction ID
- Purchase date/time
- Purchase token (Android) and/or receipt data (iOS) for verification
- Premium entitlement status (active/inactive)

LUMIO does **not** collect or store your full payment card details. Payments are processed by the app stores (Google Play / Apple App Store).

---

### 1.4 Advertising-related data (Google AdMob)
LUMIO may display ads through third-party advertising services (such as **Google AdMob**).

These third-party services may automatically collect certain technical and device-related information to provide and improve advertising, which may include (depending on device settings, region, and service behavior):
- Device identifiers
- IP address
- Approximate location (derived from IP)
- Ad interaction data (views, clicks, impressions)
- Device and app information
- Diagnostics and performance data

Please review the relevant third-party privacy policies for more information.

---

### 1.5 Technical data (automatic)
Like most mobile apps, LUMIO and/or integrated services may process limited technical information such as:
- Device type / model
- Operating system version
- App version
- Language / locale settings
- Basic diagnostics or crash-related technical signals (if generated by system/SDK behavior)

---

We use information to:

- Provide core app features (recipe suggestions, planning, shopping list, analysis, fridge matching)
- Store your preferences and app state locally
- Verify and manage premium entitlements/subscriptions
- Maintain app functionality and security
- Serve ads (where applicable)
- Improve app performance and reliability
- Prevent abuse or fraud related to premium access

- Or you uninstall the app.

Purchase/entitlement verification records may be retained for as long as reasonably necessary to:
- Validate premium access,
- Prevent fraud/abuse,

- Resolve disputes.

## 6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information processed by LUMIO. However, no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 7. Children’s Privacy

LUMIO includes content categories such as **“Bebek Mutfağı” (Baby Kitchen)** intended for recipe guidance and meal planning support, generally for parents/caregivers.

LUMIO is not designed for children to create accounts or submit personal information directly, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children through account registration (because account registration is not required).

If you believe that personal information from a child has been provided to us in error, please contact us and we will review the request.
